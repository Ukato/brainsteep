AltSpace VR Events

Altspace VR is a virtual meeting-space platform. You get an avatar and can move around in 3D worlds. You can access it with a typical computer running Windows or through a VR headset. We hold a number of different events here including live broadcasts of our podcast, Brainsteep, discussions with you guys and presentations about, you guessed it, tea and philosophy. Visit altvr.com to download the program, or find it in your VR provider's store if you're an early adoptor and have a VR headset. It's free and fairly easy to use, but has a number of issues that will hopefully eventually get fixed.
